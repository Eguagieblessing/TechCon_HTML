HISTORY

TechCon is described as Africa’s premier multi-platform technology conference, bringing together tech leaders, decision-makers, and disruptors to explore transformative technologies across sectors including government, finance, education, etc
It includes workshops, live demos, training, case studies, labs, etc., aimed especially at emerging technologies (blockchain, AI, fintech, etc.) plus policy & regulatory frameworks

MISSION

To accelerate Africa’s digital transformation via creating a platform where stakeholders from different industries can learn, collaborate, and innovate using emerging technologies
